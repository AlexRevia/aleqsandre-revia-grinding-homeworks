Frequently Asked Questions

What is ChatVerse?

ChatVerse is a platform where you can chat with AI characters created by our community or build your own. Our characters range from educational companions to entertaining personalities, storytellers, and more.

Is ChatVerse free to use?

Yes! ChatVerse is completely free to use. You can chat with any public character and create your own characters without any cost. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

How do I create my own AI character?

Creating a character is easy! Just go to the Create page and follow our step-by-step process. You'll define your character's name, personality, conversation style, and provide example conversations to train them. No coding required!

Can I make my characters private?

Absolutely! When creating a character, you can choose to make it public (anyone can chat with it), unlisted (only people with the link can chat), or private (only you can chat with it).

What technology powers the AI characters?

Our characters are powered by advanced large language models that we've fine-tuned for character interactions. Each character has its own personality profile that guides how it responds in conversations.

How can I report inappropriate content?

We take content moderation seriously. If you encounter any inappropriate content, please use the report feature on the character's profile or contact our support team directly. We review all reports promptly.
