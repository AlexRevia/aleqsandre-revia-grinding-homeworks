THE WALKING DEAD

introduction to the walking dead

walking dead is an drama, horror and thriller genre. its about a police man named rick grimes
who gets shot in a gun fight and falls into a coma. later on he wakes up in a middle of zombie apocalypse
and sees that everything is destroyed and fallen apart in the city. for now his main goal is to find his
family and survive.

Main character

Rick Grimes

Rick grimes is a very uniqe character. He stands out for his leadership and quick decision making.
He is brave, smart, and makes tough choices to keep everyone safe. Rick sometimes has to make very hard decisions
to survive and protect his loved ones. He later on becomes cold hearted and kills large amount of humans to survive(he is a good guy tho)
